Salesforce Gong DocuSign
Step-by-Step Process
1. Lead assigned to SDR/AE→ Lead record
2. Discovery call scheduled→ Meeting booked
3. Discovery call conducted→ Gong: recording, transcript, key moments
4. Lead → Opportunity + Contact + Account→ Opportunity created
5. Demo delivered→ Gong: demo recording

7. Negotiation (if needed)→ Objection handling

CRITICAL: CRITICAL HANDOFF PROBLEM

What happens:

Onboarding gets a multi-line text field. No structured data about use case, stakeholders, expectations, or competitive context.

Impact:

Onboarding kickoff call REPEATS sales discovery. Customer is frustrated. "Didn't we already tell you this?"

Fix in progress: Gong → PlanHat integration will surface pre-sale conversations to onboarding team
